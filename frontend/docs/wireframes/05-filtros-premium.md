# Wireframe: Filtros Premium

## Elements:
- **Navigation/Header:**
  - Back button.
  - Progress bar (Step 4 of X).

- **Main Content Area:**
  - Title: "Enhance Your Trip with Premium Filters"
  - List of available premium filters.

  - Button: "Continue to Add-ons" (or "Review Trip" if no add-ons are applicable)
